How It Works

Focus. Hatch. Collect.

Cracked motivates you to stay focused by rewarding uninterrupted study sessions with collectible animals.

🥚

Choose an Egg

Pick from Farm, Jungle, or Arctic eggs — each containing unique animals at different rarities.

⏱️

Set Your Timer

Choose your study duration and start a focus session. A Live Activity tracks your progress right on the lock screen.

🧘

Stay Focused

Keep the app active while you study. Leave the app and your egg cracks — stay focused and it hatches!

🎉

Hatch & Collect

Complete the session to hatch a random animal. Earn coins, grow your sanctuary, and climb the leaderboard.

Features

Everything You Need to Stay Focused

Built for students and anyone who wants to build better focus habits through positive reinforcement.

⏱️

Flexible Focus Timer

Set sessions from a few minutes to hours with an intuitive circular time picker. Earn 5 coins per minute studied.

🏝️

Animal Sanctuary

All hatched animals live in your personal sanctuary — a scrollable grid you can browse and admire anytime.

🛒

Egg Shop

Spend earned coins on new eggs. Farm (100), Jungle (250), and Arctic (500) — each with unique animal pools.

🔥

Study Streaks

Maintain daily streaks for bonus coins and free eggs. Hit milestones at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days for big rewards.

🏆

Leaderboards

Compete globally or with friends across categories: Animals Hatched, Study Time, Streak, and Total Coins.

📱

Live Activities

Track your active timer directly on the lock screen and Dynamic Island — no need to open the app.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your progress automatically syncs across all your devices via iCloud. Pick up right where you left off.

👥

Social & Friends

Add friends, compare progress, and appear on each other's leaderboards. Study better together.

🐷

Piggybank Mode

Use the Piggybank for double coin earning (10 coins/min) — but be careful, it can shatter if you leave!

Privacy & Data

Your Data, Your Control

Cracked is designed with privacy at its core. We collect minimal data and keep everything secure.

🔐

Sign In with Apple

Optional sign-in via Apple's privacy-preserving authentication. Your Apple ID credentials are stored securely in the device Keychain.

☁️

iCloud Sync Only

Game progress syncs through your personal iCloud account. We don't operate external servers or collect personal data.

📱

On-Device Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is shared with third parties.

🛡️

No Tracking

No analytics SDKs, no user tracking, no behavioral profiling. Your study habits are yours alone.
