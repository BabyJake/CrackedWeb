Family Controls Capability Request

About Our App

CrackedSwift is a gamified study timer app that helps users stay focused and productive. Users set study timers, and upon successful completion, they hatch virtual animals as rewards. The app encourages consistent study habits through engaging gameplay mechanics.

Why We Need Family Controls

We are requesting the Family Controls (Distribution) capability to provide parents and guardians with tools to help manage their children's study time and device usage.

Primary Use Case: Parents can set study schedules and monitor their children's study sessions, ensuring productive use of screen time while maintaining a healthy balance between learning and other activities.

Specific features we plan to implement:

  • Study Time Scheduling: Allow parents to set designated study times when the app can be used
  • Session Monitoring: Help parents track their children's study session completion
  • Screen Time Integration: Integrate with Screen Time to provide comprehensive usage insights
  • Parental Controls: Enable parents to set study goals and track progress

App Information

  • Bundle ID: Cracked.CrackedSwift
  • Platform: iOS
  • Category: Education / Productivity
  • Target Audience: Students and families

Privacy & Safety

We are committed to user privacy and safety. Family Controls will be implemented with:

  • Full transparency about data collection and usage
  • Compliance with COPPA and other child privacy regulations
  • Secure data handling and storage
  • Parental consent requirements for all family features
